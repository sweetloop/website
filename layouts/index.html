Sweet Loop Cafe is a locally-owned and operated dessert and drink cafe near Wilfrid Laurier University and the University of Waterloo Campus. It's the only dessert cafe in Waterloo Region making handcrafted churros.

Also serving extra thick soft serve ice cream and a full menu of drinks (espresso, matcha, hojicha, tea, and hot chocolate), there's a dessert for everyone and every occasion—or no occasion at all!

Using only the best ingredients—like ceremonial-grade matcha sourced directly from a farm in South Korea—Sweet Loop desserts and drinks will bring a little joy to your day!
